# Fixed Stars

Use this file when the user asks about 恒星, fixed stars, Regulus, Spica, Algol, Antares, Sirius, Fomalhaut, or when a chart has tight star contacts to angles, luminaries, or chart rulers.

## Use Boundary

Fixed stars are modifiers, not the base of a reading. They become important when tightly conjunct:

- Ascendant, MC, Descendant, IC.
- Sun or Moon.
- Ascendant ruler, MC ruler, Lot of Fortune ruler, or a topic ruler central to the question.
- A planet already prominent by angularity, rulership, or timing activation.

Prefer conjunctions within 1°. Be cautious with wider orbs. Recalculate star positions for the native's epoch or use a reliable chart program; do not rely on 2000-era positions for exact work.

## Judgment Method

1. Identify the star and its planetary nature, such as Mars-Jupiter or Venus-Mercury.
2. Judge the natal planet or point first by ordinary chart rules.
3. Let the star intensify, color, or specialize that existing testimony.
4. Check whether the star's nature agrees with or strains the planet it joins.
5. Avoid dramatic claims unless the same theme repeats in houses, rulers, aspects, and timing.

Example: Regulus on MC can strengthen ambition, rank, command, and public visibility. It does not by itself guarantee high office; the 10th lord, Sun, MC condition, sect, benefics/malefics, and timing must agree.

## Selected Star Notes

- Regulus / 轩辕十四: Mars-Jupiter nature. Rank, ambition, command, martial or royal visibility. Can elevate strongly when linked to angles or luminaries; pride or fall follows if the chart shows hubris and retaliation.
- Spica / 角宿一: Venus-Mars nature. Protection, talent, art, science, wealth or honor. One of the more helpful stars when tightly joined to a significator.
- Algol / 大陵五: traditionally difficult. Intensifies crisis, violence, loss of control, head/neck symbolism, or extreme states when other testimonies agree. Do not sensationalize it in client work.
- Aldebaran / 毕宿五: Mars nature. Courage, eloquence, public force, military or competitive visibility; success may require integrity and restraint.
- Antares / 心宿二: Mars-Jupiter nature. Honor, intensity, warfare, legal/strategic contests, dramatic success and possible reversal.
- Sirius / 天狼星: Jupiter-Mars nature. Heat, ambition, wealth, prominence, passionate drive. Strong on MC or luminaries when the chart supports fame.
- Fomalhaut / 北落师门: Venus-Mercury nature. Vision, art, science, spiritualization of material aims; can swing strongly good or bad depending on the planet and chart.
- Arcturus / 大角: Jupiter-Mars nature. Advancement through initiative, travel, leadership, and sustained effort.
- Vega / 织女星: Mercury-Venus nature. Artistic, musical, political, literary, or refined talent; reputation can be bright but may be temporary without broader support.
- Procyon / 南河三: Mercury-Mars nature. Quick rise, action, impatience, sudden success and decline if discipline is weak.
- Scheat / 室宿二 and Markab / 室宿一: Mars-Mercury nature. Writing, art, legal or technical ability can appear; difficult charts may show danger, confinement, water or accident symbolism.

## Consultation Language

For private readings, translate harsh star lore into grounded language:

- "This intensifies the need for caution around conflict and risk."
- "The chart favors visibility, but it also asks for restraint and ethical handling of power."
- "This star adds technical/artistic force to an already prominent planet."

Do not predict death, murder, disgrace, or disaster from a fixed star alone.
