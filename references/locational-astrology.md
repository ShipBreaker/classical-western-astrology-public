# Locational Astrology / ACG

Use this file for ACG, astrocartography, Astro*Carto*Graphy, relocation chart, relocated chart, local space, 行星线, 迁移择地, 留学地点, 移民, 搬家, 旅行, 远距触发, and direction-based place questions.

## Status Of The Technique

ACG and related locational techniques are modern supplements. They can be useful for relocation, travel, study abroad, work locations, public events, and relationship geography, but they do not override the natal chart.

The natal planet's condition remains the root. A Venus line cannot guarantee happiness if natal Venus is heavily afflicted; it may bring Venus themes together with Venus problems.

## Astrocartography / ACG

ACG maps where natal planets were angular on Earth at the birth moment.

The usual lines show where a planet is:

- Rising / Ascendant.
- Setting / Descendant.
- Culminating / MC.
- Anti-culminating / IC.

The simple core matters more than over-differentiating the four line types: a planet line marks a region where that planet's natal energy becomes visible. If a software or user asks for AC/DC/MC/IC distinctions, use them, but keep the natal condition first.

## Reading A Planet Line

Judge a line by:

- The planet's natal dignity, sect, house, rulership, aspects, and reception.
- The topic being asked: love, work, study, health, family, money, visibility, retreat.
- Whether the line is near the actual city or realistic travel/residence area.
- Whether the line repeats a natal promise already shown by ordinary houses.

Examples:

- Sun line: visibility, leadership, recognition, father/authority themes; can also intensify ego pressure.
- Moon line: family, public contact, bodily rhythm, home, women, changeability.
- Mercury line: study, language, trade, writing, transport, teaching, networking.
- Venus line: love, pleasure, art, ease, social help; can also laziness, indulgence, relationship disappointment if natal Venus is difficult.
- Mars line: action, competition, conflict, tools, surgery, accidents, military; useful for drive, risky for peace.
- Jupiter line: opportunity, teachers, law, growth, foreigners, protection; can overexpand.
- Saturn line: duty, career structure, isolation, age, endurance, heaviness, delay.
- Uranus/Neptune/Pluto lines: optional modern modifiers for rupture/freedom, dream/confusion/spirituality, crisis/power/depth.

## Parans And Line Intersections

Parans or crossing effects show where two planetary angular lines combine, often by latitude.

Practical rules:

- A crossing or paran can work within about one degree of latitude as a rough guide.
- Ignore crossings near the poles or places the person cannot realistically live, visit, or interact with.
- Blend the two planets by natal condition and topic. Mars/Uranus can show accident, violence, rupture, technical urgency, or breakthrough action depending context. Moon/Jupiter can show public support, family growth, popularity, or emotional excess.

## Remote Activation

ACG can sometimes describe places tied to a person's public events even if the person does not physically go there, especially for leaders, institutions, and high-visibility figures.

For ordinary consultations, treat remote activation as secondary. Use it more for:

- Public figures and political events.
- Companies, factories, offices, branches, or markets tied to the native.
- Partners, clients, or people from a location.
- Media/public impact connected with a place.

## Relocation Chart

A relocation chart keeps the birth time and zodiacal planet positions, but recalculates the houses and angles for the new city.

Use it for "Should I live/study/work in this city?" questions.

Judge:

- New Ascendant and MC.
- Which natal planets become angular.
- Whether topic houses improve or worsen: 2nd money, 5th romance/children, 7th partners, 9th study abroad, 10th career, 11th patrons/audience, 4th home.
- Whether a desired improvement creates a new cost elsewhere. A city can improve romance but worsen money, or improve career while increasing stress.

Relocation changes the field of manifestation; it does not remove the natal promise.

## Local Space

Local Space maps planetary directions from the current place. Use as a light, optional technique:

- Venus direction for love, art, pleasure, social ease.
- Moon direction for home, comfort, family feeling.
- Jupiter direction for study, teachers, law, religion, travel, growth.
- Mercury direction for study, communication, commerce.
- Mars direction for sport, action, competition, but also conflict.
- Saturn direction for duty, work, discipline, but also heaviness.

It can be applied to city directions, travel routes, or even home layout, but do not overpromise it. Treat indoor placement rules as experimental symbolic feng shui rather than a core astrology judgment.

## Practical Use Cases

Use ACG/relocation/local space for:

- Choosing among study-abroad schools.
- Choosing a city for work, factory, office, market, or public activity.
- Comparing countries for immigration or long stay.
- Finding places more supportive for love, family, career, retreat, creativity, or research.
- Understanding why a place felt unusually good, heavy, chaotic, lonely, or productive.

Always include real-world constraints: visa, language, money, job market, safety, climate, family, medical access, legal rules, and the person's actual choices.

## Output Shape For Place Questions

1. Natal place promise: whether relocation/travel is supported.
2. ACG lines near each candidate place.
3. Relocation chart changes for each place.
4. Local Space direction if relevant.
5. Practical ranking by topic.
6. Boundaries and non-astrological constraints.
