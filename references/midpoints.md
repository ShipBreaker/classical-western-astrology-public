# Midpoints / 中点

Use this file for 中点, midpoint, 45-degree dial, 90-degree dial, Cosmobiology, Hamburg School, Ebertin, Noel Tyl, and midpoint contacts in Solar Arc, synastry, or natal analysis.

## Status Of The Technique

Midpoints are a modern technique. They can be accurate, but they are complex and easy to overuse. In this classical skill, use them as a focused supplement after the natal house/ruler structure is clear.

Do not replace dignity, house rulership, reception, sect, or natal promise with midpoint cookbook meanings.

## Basic Notation

`A = B/C` means A contacts the midpoint of B and C.

In the common 45-degree system, the contact can be:

- Conjunction to the midpoint.
- Opposition to the midpoint.
- Square to the midpoint.
- Semi-square to the midpoint.
- Sesqui-square to the midpoint.

These are treated as one relationship in midpoint notation. Do not interpret the aspect type itself as good or bad. The point is that A is connected to the B/C blend.

## Interpretation Formula

Read `A = B/C` as:

- A is the visible outlet, function, person, angle, or life field.
- B/C is the combined symbolic field, as if B and C were blended.
- The result is not automatically fortunate or unfortunate. It describes the image, activity, or social correspondence.

Example:

- Sun = Mercury/Neptune can show identity or life purpose expressed through imagination, media, image, dream, inspiration, film, illusion, or subtle communication.
- MC = Mercury/Neptune can describe a career or public role involving media, film, symbolic language, dream analysis, imagination, counseling, or ambiguous communication.

Always choose the concrete form by natal houses, biography, and context.

## Priority Points

Do not read all possible midpoint structures by default. Prioritize:

- MC: public role, vocation, visible career, reputation. Only high priority when birth time is reliable.
- Sun: identity, vitality, life purpose, self-definition.
- Moon: emotional need, body rhythm, family habit, public mood.
- Ascendant: body, presentation, immediate life path; birth-time sensitive.
- Aries Point / AP: public-world contact, collective visibility, how a person appears in the world. More useful for public figures or mundane charts than ordinary private questions.
- Sun/Moon midpoint: relationship integration, inner marriage of will and need; important in synastry and timing.

For ordinary clients, MC and Sun are usually enough. AP and the full 90-degree dial are optional.

## What To Ignore First

To avoid midpoint overload:

- Do not interpret every one of the many possible midpoints.
- Three inner/personal factors together can be ordinary unless repeated elsewhere.
- Three slow outer factors together are usually collective or mundane rather than personal, unless they contact the Sun, Moon, angles, Ascendant ruler, MC ruler, or a topic ruler.
- AP midpoint structures often matter most when the person has public impact; for private life, keep them secondary.

## Useful Pattern Groups

Use these as pattern hints, not fixed fate:

- Mercury/Neptune: imagination, image, film, television, dream language, inspired or confused thought.
- Uranus/Neptune: occult, mystical, psychological, spiritual, visionary, unusual invisible worlds.
- Mercury/Pluto: depth psychology, research, investigation, persuasive or obsessive thought.
- Mercury/Saturn: structured thought, serious writing, mental labor, practical analysis.
- Jupiter/Uranus: discovery, invention, exploration, breakthrough, adventure, expansion through novelty.
- Mars/Saturn: hard labor, endurance, pain, obstruction, discipline, violence or athletic struggle depending context.
- Saturn/Neptune: weakness, illness, fatigue, loss of certainty, invisible burden, spiritual discipline when constructive.
- Sun/Jupiter/Pluto combinations: success, influence, large-scale growth, wealth or power when tied to MC, 10th, 2nd, 8th, 11th, or Fortune.
- Venus/Mars midpoint: attraction, sex, romantic or erotic activity; judge by 5th/7th and timing.

Use "social correspondence": check how the symbolism appears in a person's real work, public role, or repeated life activity.

## Midpoints In Synastry

Use sparingly:

- Another person's planet on the native's Sun/Moon midpoint can strongly activate relationship feeling.
- Malefics or hard modern planets on the Sun/Moon midpoint can show relational stress, rupture, frustration, or painful growth.
- Another person's planet on Venus/Mars can show sexual attraction or erotic irritation.

Do not judge the whole relationship from one midpoint contact. Read both natal charts and ordinary synastry first.

## Midpoints In Timing

Solar Arc or slow transits to natal midpoints can trigger events when natal promise supports them.

Practical rules:

- Solar Arc contacts to natal midpoints are more useful than chasing every fast transit.
- Slow transits can matter when they hit Sun/Moon, Venus/Mars, Saturn/Neptune, or career/status midpoints.
- Fast transits to midpoints are usually too quick for forecasting unless confirming a known date.
- A midpoint trigger must be confirmed by natal promise and another timing testimony.

For relationship timing, Solar Arc or slow transit to Sun/Moon midpoint can coincide with meeting, separation, marriage pressure, or relationship change, but it needs support from 5th/7th/relationship timing.

## Consultation Boundaries

Midpoints are excellent for research and pattern recognition, but risky in live consultation if used too broadly.

When using them with a client:

- Name only the few midpoint structures that repeat the main natal story.
- Explain the image, not a deterministic event.
- Give the constructive expression of a difficult midpoint.
- If the midpoint contradicts the main natal chart, keep it as a minor note.
