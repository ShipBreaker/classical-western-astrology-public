# Profession Signatures / 职业取象

Use this file for career-path questions, professional aptitude, job change, industry selection, vocational case analysis, and questions that ask "what work is this chart suited for?"

## Method

Read profession by job function, not by job title. Translate the actual work into astrological grammar:

1. Identify the work's core action: compete, speak, design, heal, negotiate, manage, counsel, investigate, teach, perform, build, or handle crisis.
2. Assign natural planets to the action.
3. Assign houses to the work environment, clients, tools, money channel, platform, and daily routine.
4. Check whether the Ascendant, Ascendant ruler, MC, 10th ruler, luminaries, and relevant topic houses repeat the same structure.
5. Use signs for style, not as the sole vocational determinant.
6. Separate entry-level suitability from long-term rank. A person may have the skill signature but lack client flow, platform, or public support.
7. Use timing to judge when the profession becomes active: the same natal structure may appear as study, internship, job entry, promotion, entrepreneurship, or public breakthrough at different periods.

## Career Structure Layers

- Core capacity: Ascendant, Ascendant ruler, Sun, Moon, angular planets, and strongest dignified planets.
- Vocation and platform: MC, 10th house, 10th ruler, Sun, Saturn for authority/structure, Jupiter for institutions/status, and planets in the 10th.
- Daily work and craft: 6th house, 6th ruler, Mercury for skill learning, Mars for execution, Saturn for repetition and technical discipline.
- Clients and opponents: 7th house and 7th ruler.
- Money channel: 2nd, 8th, 11th, Fortune, benefics, and the house that receives the career action.
- Public spread: 3rd for writing/speech/media mechanics, 9th for doctrine/law/higher teaching, 11th for audience/network/platform.
- Hidden or behind-the-scenes work: 12th, cadent planets, Neptune as modern supplement, and planets that act out of public view.

## Sports And Physical Performance

Sports require Mars, but the sport type decides which planet extends Mars.

- General athlete: Mars for action and competition, 6th for training, 7th for opponent, 10th for rank, 11th for audience/team/support.
- Endurance or long match play: Mars with Jupiter for stamina and reach; Saturn for persistence and repeatable form.
- Precision sport: Mars with Saturn and Mercury. Saturn stabilizes the body and form; Mercury reads angles, timing, tactics, and small adjustments.
- Extreme or high-risk sport: Mars with Jupiter for expansion, Saturn for control, Uranus for explosive novelty, Pluto for risk confrontation. Neptune can blur form or timing unless the chart has strong stabilizers.
- Creative athletic movement: add the 5th house for performance, choreography, and expressive variation.

Specific patterns:

- Tennis: Mars plus 6th training and 7th opponent. 3rd house and Mercury describe hand-eye coordination, shot pattern, and tactical reading. Earth signs emphasize endurance and heavy repetition; air signs technique and adjustment; fire signs serve and attack; water signs fine control and changeable rhythm.
- Freestyle skiing: Mars is central, but Mars-Jupiter and Mars-Saturn are both needed. Jupiter gives extension and aerial variation; Saturn gives landing control and risk discipline. 5th house adds creative movement; 6th daily training; 7th competition; 8th or Pluto may appear where injury/risk psychology is central.
- Snooker/billiards: Mars must become precise rather than merely forceful. Mars in water/earth signs, Mars-Saturn, Mars-Jupiter, and Mercury/Saturn structures support fine motor control, layout planning, restraint, and long-match concentration. 3rd house is important for hand skill, calculation, and tactical review.

## Film, Voice, Stage, And Performance Work

Performance professions need a bridge between personal expression, craft, and audience reception.

- Director: Venus for art, Neptune for film/dream-image as a modern supplement, Mercury for script and coordination, Mars for execution, Saturn for command and management. 5th house is creative authorship; 12th house can show behind-the-scenes image-making; 10th house and luminaries decide visibility and rank.
- Voice actor: Mercury for speech, Venus for artistic tone, Moon for emotional receptivity, Mars for vocal force/effect, Jupiter for range, Uranus for quick shifts and unusual voice. 2nd house describes throat/voice resource; 3rd speech; 5th performance; 12th behind-the-scenes work.
- Stand-up comedian: Mercury must be strong, activated, or sharply configured. Mars adds timing, interruption, boldness, and physical delivery; Jupiter range and worldview; Uranus reversal and surprise; Pluto depth and taboo insight; Moon empathy and life observation; Sun stage identity. 3rd house is writing/speech, 5th performance and original material, 11th audience, 9th social viewpoint. Hard aspects can be productive because comedy often converts tension, pain, contradiction, or offense into structure and release.

## Digital Media, Writing, And Technical Work

Digital and content professions often mix craft skill, platform circulation, client conversion, and fast iteration. Separate the role's visible title from its actual mechanism: speaking, writing, coding, selling, designing systems, or building an audience.

- E-commerce streamer / 电商主播: Mercury is the speech, script, sales explanation, and real-time response planet. Venus improves screen image and product appeal; Mars gives speed, interruptions, reaction, and live-room pressure tolerance. Saturn supports team management, operational discipline, and compliance; Jupiter/Uranus help brand incubation, cross-field expansion, and new platform formats; Sun/Moon with dignity or strong public testimony help head-level IP. 3rd house is speaking/script/workflow knowledge; 11th audience and fan aggregation; 2nd product selection, price sense, and goods; 7th customers, suppliers, and brands; 6th live-room operations and team routine; 8th discounts, conversion, outside capital, and shared profit; 10th public status and management turn. Timing matters strongly because the front-stage role can exhaust the body and sleep rhythm; read whether the chart later shifts to operations, training, brand ownership, or management.
- Web novelist / 网络小说作家: Mercury is the writing engine. Jupiter adds knowledge storage, genre range, and publication reach; Uranus/Neptune add world-building, fantasy, sci-fi, image flow, and unconventional settings; Moon adds narrative empathy, observation, reader resonance, and emotional release. 3rd house is daily writing and serialized output; 5th plot creation and characters; 7th reader response and relationship mirroring; 9th deep knowledge, long-form doctrine, publishing, and copyright expansion; 11th subscribers, fan groups, and platform ranking; 12th hidden worlds, mystery, loneliness, and psychological material. The Ascendant, Ascendant ruler, Moon, and 6th must be checked for stamina because the work demands long solitary repetition. Fixed signs help persistence; mutable signs help market sensitivity and serial adjustment. Fire leans battle/adventure, water emotion/romance/psychology, earth history/material realism, and air structure, dialogue, or large conceptual systems. For top authors, require a larger pattern: audience adhesion, benefic or luminary support, income/copyright channels, and timing that activates the writing structure.
- Programmer / 程序员: Mercury is primary for logic, language, abstraction, debugging, and technical learning. 3rd house supports study and code syntax; 6th supports practice, implementation, testing, maintenance, and repeated bug fixing. Saturn gives architecture, stability, constraints, long-term systems, and deployment discipline; Jupiter gives large systems, theory, open platforms, and institutional or academic reach; Mars gives implementation speed, troubleshooting, refactoring pressure, and incident response. 7th is requirements, product negotiation, and stakeholder interface; 9th is computer theory, algorithms, higher education, and architecture principles; 10th is management, technical authority, and public title; 5th/11th support creative engineering, AI, cloud-native, open source, blockchain, and platform innovation. Earth and fixed signs favor deep specialization; air favors systems and abstraction; fire/cardinal signatures favor new fields, startup momentum, and technical leadership. Distinguish technical-depth tracks from management tracks: the former needs Mercury/Saturn/Jupiter/6th-9th continuity; the latter needs Sun/Saturn/10th and people-structure testimony.

## Design, Beauty, Space, And Material Craft

Design work is not Venus alone. It requires taste plus translation into usable form.

- Fashion designer: Venus for aesthetics, Mercury for planning and design language, Mars for cutting/handwork, Moon for body/feminine/public taste, Sun/Mars for masculine or sharper style, Saturn for pattern discipline and production constraints. 5th house governs creative concept; 6th garment/craft execution; 9th cultural reference and design doctrine.
- Interior designer: Venus and Mercury are primary. Saturn adds construction, material, budget, and project management; Moon helps understand living needs; Mars executes and coordinates workers. 4th house is home/space, 5th design concept, 6th construction execution, 7th client demand. Strong 1st/10th or 7th/10th ties help independent studios and partnership-based business.
- Illustrator / 插画师: Venus is the core aesthetic planet and Neptune is a strong modern supplement for image, atmosphere, fantasy, and visual imagination. Mercury helps style analysis, client translation, digital tools, and teaching; Moon adds public taste, children's art, emotional tone, and image receptivity; Jupiter/Uranus/Pluto can enlarge the style into mythic, experimental, or intense forms. 3rd house supports art learning, visual language, software, and communication; 5th original creation; 6th craft repetition and "many hours" practice; 12th inspiration, dream image, and unseen material. Independent illustrators require 10th-house career visibility, 7th-house client stickiness, and often 11th-house internet audience/fan conversion. Hard aspects and retrograde planets should not be dismissed: when tied to Venus/Neptune/Mercury/5th, they can create a recognizable visual impact or unusual style. Do not promise a short-training career without natal promise through the Ascendant, Ascendant ruler, Venus/Mercury/5th/6th, and timing activation.

## Medicine, Animals, Death, And Crisis Services

Some careers use difficult houses and malefics constructively. Do not treat debility, retrogradation, cadency, or out-of-sect condition as automatic failure; in niche, unpleasant, hidden, or taboo fields, these conditions can describe the work's non-mainstream nature.

- Traditional Chinese medicine practitioner / 中医师: Read this as medicine plus doctrine, patient care, hidden causes, and long clinical apprenticeship. Moon can show nursing, body-fluid rhythms, constitution, and patient care; Mercury/Jupiter show formulas, herbs, diagnosis logic, medical texts, and teaching lineage; Mars can show moxibustion, heat, stimulation, surgery-like intervention, or acute work; Saturn can show bones, chronic disease, rehabilitation, restraint, and long practice; Venus can show acupuncture, harmonizing method, and body balance. 6th house is illness, daily clinical service, and healing routine; 8th severe disease, crisis, mortality, and risk; 12th invisible causes, hospitals, hidden suffering, retreat, and occult/medical overlap; 9th classics, doctrine, master-disciple learning, research, and international/academic transmission. 2nd can join through diet therapy; 3rd/11th through health education, culture spread, and public content; 7th through doctor-patient relationship and independent clinics; 10th through medical status. Water/earth emphasis helps patience and containment. For a high-level physician, look for benefic or noble testimony, access to teachers, and repeated links among Ascendant/10th/6th/8th/12th/9th; for midlife career change, a 6th/8th/12th activation tied to the 10th can turn crisis into healing work.
- Pet doctor / veterinarian: Mars for surgery and invasive treatment; Mercury for diagnostics, imaging, and lab analysis; Saturn for restraint, protocol, animal handling, and chronic care. 6th house is pets/small animals and daily clinical work; 8th disease, surgery, crisis, and mortality; 12th large animals, confinement, hospital-like conditions, hidden suffering; 7th owners/clients.
- Funeral industry: 8th house is death and post-death matters; 12th after-death/invisible/ritual or secluded spaces; 4th burial, land, graves, and family endings; 3rd/9th ritual speech, religious or legal ceremony, and meaning-making. Mars and Saturn handle body, fire, bone, preservation, and procedure; Mercury/Venus/Moon support counseling, family communication, ritual design, and grief care.
- Health and crisis reading: a profession can work with 6th/8th/12th topics without implying the native personally suffers the topic. Distinguish "I handle this matter for others" from "this happens to my body/life" by rulership, reception, and whether the Ascendant/luminaries are harmed or empowered.

## Divination, Counseling, And Spiritual Services

- Tarot/divination counselor: Mercury translates symbols into language; Moon gives empathy and emotional attunement; Jupiter gives meaning, guidance, and symbolic breadth; Neptune can add image-sensitivity and intuition as a modern modifier; Venus helps with art products and client experience. 3rd house is the act of reading and speaking, 7th client relationship, 9th doctrine/system, 12th subconscious and invisible material, 11th audience/self-media.
- Astrologer / 占星师: In classical practice Mercury is the primary significator because the work requires learning, calculation, interpretation, writing, speech, and teaching. Uranus can be used as a modern supplement for astrology, astronomy, technical systems, reform, and unconventional knowledge. 7th house is essential for one-to-one clients and market trust; 9th for doctrine, certification, higher study, publishing, and teaching; 12th for intuition, hidden causes, dreams, and invisible material; 3rd for content, courses, short writing, and consultation language; 11th for audience/community; 8th for paid transformation work and clients' resources; 10th for public role and personal IP. Moon/Neptune improve empathy and inspiration; Jupiter broadens cross-disciplinary tools and philosophical frame; Sun helps public authority and brand. Fire-style astrologers energize and encourage; earth/air styles analyze and advise pragmatically; water styles support emotional and therapeutic work. For specialization, route the same astrology skill through the relevant houses: career counseling needs 9th/10th, parenting or family education needs 3rd/5th/4th, medical or crisis astrology needs 6th/8th/12th, content monetization needs 3rd/11th/7th/8th. Many astrologers begin during difficult life periods; confirm the calling by natal promise and timing rather than by temporary distress alone.
- Yoga teacher: Mars for physical practice; Jupiter for extension, philosophy, and teaching lineage; Saturn for discipline, posture, and long practice; Mercury for instruction. 6th house rules health, training, and daily body work; 3rd/9th teaching; 12th meditation, retreat, breath, and spiritual discipline; 7th students/clients.

## Law, Procurement, Recruiting, And Negotiation

These professions use Mercury differently depending on whether the work is legal argument, buying, or matching people.

- Lawyer: Jupiter is law and institutions; Mercury is legal writing, research, documents, and argument; Sun/Moon improve platform, rank, and client level. 7th house is clients, opponents, litigation, contracts, and negotiation; 9th law/jurisprudence; 3rd writing and communication. 2nd/8th emphasis can point toward commercial, financial, inheritance, or compensation cases; 12th toward criminal, confinement, hidden matters, or institutional disputes; 10th toward firm-building, public role, or corporate legal work.
- Procurement: Mercury gathers information, compares prices, writes contracts, and communicates; Saturn manages suppliers, inventory, rules, and cost control. 3rd house is inquiry/data/document flow; 7th suppliers and one-to-one negotiation; 8th bargaining, discounts, other people's money, and risk transfer; 11th as 2nd from the 10th can show company resources; 6th supply chain and daily operational coordination.
- Headhunter/recruiter: Mercury is the information channel and matching mechanism; Venus/Moon add social intelligence, rapport, and emotional reading; Saturn helps retain long-term client relationships; Jupiter/Sun/Moon can raise the client level. 3rd house is search and communication, 7th target person/client, 9th industry knowledge and legal awareness, 11th network and referrals. Mutable signs help movement and search; fixed signs help retention; cardinal signs help development and opening new accounts.

## Style By Element

Use element emphasis to describe style after the vocational structure is already present:

- Fire: speed, risk, attack, stage heat, confidence, self-display.
- Earth: form, repetition, material, craft, endurance, reliability.
- Air: language, concept, adjustment, networking, tactics, abstraction.
- Water: sensitivity, image, mood, fine control, empathy, psychological depth.

## Niche And Non-Mainstream Work

Retrograde, out-of-sect, debilitated, cadent, or hidden planets can mark unusual routes, niche fields, taboo work, behind-the-scenes roles, foreign systems, or work ignored by mainstream society. Read these factors as context, not as automatic weakness. They are useful when repeated by the 6th/8th/12th, the 10th ruler, or the Ascendant ruler.

## Timing Career Activation

- 3rd/9th activation: study, certification, writing, outreach, training, cross-field learning, publishing.
- 6th activation: daily workload, apprenticeship, service role, craft repetition, health/body routines.
- 7th activation: clients, contracts, competition, negotiation, partnership, market response.
- 10th activation: title, public role, career decision, leadership, visible result.
- 11th activation: audience, network, patrons, referrals, platform gains.
- 2nd/8th activation: income model, pricing, client money, debt, investment, compensation, business capital.

For career changes, require both natal promise and timing activation. A period can show the same structure at different levels: learning the skill, entering the industry, becoming visible, managing others, founding a business, or teaching the method.
