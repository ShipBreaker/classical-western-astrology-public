# House-Lord Flight / 宫主飞星

## Core Idea

宫主飞星 is event logic. A planet carries the topics of the house it rules into the house where it is placed. The ruled house is the source; the occupied house is the arena where that source acts.

Read it as:

`source house lord -> target house = source topics enter target arena`

Then judge whether the result is helpful or harmful by:

- dignity/debility of the lord
- angularity and accidental strength
- benefic/malefic condition
- aspects and reception
- whether the lord is under pressure from malefics, combustion, retrograde, or severe affliction
- whether the same topic is repeated elsewhere

Do not memorize a fixed sentence and stop. The sentence changes when the lord is dignified, afflicted, received, angular, cadent, or tied to another house.

## Source Houses

- 1R: self, body, temperament, agency, the native's life force.
- 2R: personal money, movable assets, food, income, cash flow.
- 3R: siblings, relatives, communication, writing, learning, short travel, neighbors.
- 4R: family root, father/parents, house, land, property, private foundation.
- 5R: children, romance, pleasure, art, games, speculation, creativity.
- 6R: illness, labor, service, subordinates, daily work, burdens.
- 7R: spouse, partners, clients, open enemies, contracts, competitors.
- 8R: death/crisis, debt, shared money, inheritance, fear, injury, partner's assets.
- 9R: higher learning, religion, law, foreign places, long travel, teachers, publishing.
- 10R: career, authority, title, rank, public action, superior, reputation.
- 11R: friends, patrons, groups, hopes, gains from career, social benefit.
- 12R: hidden enemies, confinement, hospitals, isolation, sorrow, secret trouble, retreat.

## Target Houses

- Into 1st: the source topic enters the self/body/life path and becomes personally prominent.
- Into 2nd: the source topic becomes a money/resource channel or expense.
- Into 3rd: the source topic manifests through speech, study, siblings, local movement, media.
- Into 4th: the source topic affects home, land, family, parents, foundations.
- Into 5th: the source topic enters romance, children, pleasure, art, speculation.
- Into 6th: the source topic becomes labor, illness, service, conflict with subordinates, burden.
- Into 7th: the source topic appears through spouse, partners, clients, opponents, contracts.
- Into 8th: the source topic enters debt, fear, shared assets, crisis, inheritance, loss of control.
- Into 9th: the source topic links to foreign places, religion, law, higher education, teachers.
- Into 10th: the source topic becomes public, career-related, visible, judged by authority.
- Into 11th: the source topic comes through friends, organizations, patrons, gains, hopes.
- Into 12th: the source topic becomes hidden, isolated, costly, confined, spiritual, or self-undoing.

## First-House Examples From The Transcribed Flight Lessons

Use these ruler-placement patterns as practical templates. Correct overly harsh or simplistic phrasing before applying them to consultation.

- 2R -> 1st: money and resources enter the native's life directly. If dignified, the native can attract resources or profit through personal effort/body/name. If afflicted, money anxieties, expense, or value problems attach to the self.
- 3R -> 1st: siblings, relatives, communication, study, writing, and short travel become personally important. If dignified, communication/learning ability and help from siblings/relatives improve. If afflicted, relatives, traffic, speech, or study can disturb the native.
- 4R -> 1st: family, father/parents, property, land, ancestral base, and home enter the native's life strongly. If dignified, family/property supports the native. If afflicted, family or father burdens the native.
- 5R -> 1st: children, pleasure, romance, creativity, games, and speculation enter the native's identity. Dignity gives joy, creativity, and child-related support; affliction can show romantic trouble, gambling/speculative risk, or burdens through children.
- 6R -> 1st: illness, labor, service, and burdens enter the body/life. If afflicted, this is a classic health/labor pressure signature. If dignified, it can show skill, work capacity, service competence, or technical labor, while still requiring effort.
- 7R -> 1st: spouse, partners, clients, opponents, and contracts enter the native's life. Dignity/reception can show help through partner/client or strong relationship fortune; affliction can show open enemies, relationship conflict, or partner-caused trouble.
- 8R -> 1st: crisis, debt, fear, shared assets, inheritance, injury, or mortality themes enter the body/life. This is serious when afflicted and repeated by health/crisis houses. If dignified, it may show benefit through shared money, inheritance, business capital, or crisis-management work.
- 9R -> 1st: foreign places, long travel, religion, law, higher education, and teachers enter the native's life. Dignity gives benefit through study, teachers, religion/law, publishing, travel, or foreign trade; affliction brings loss or trouble through those areas.
- 10R -> 1st: career, rank, authority, title, and public life attach strongly to identity. Dignity gives leadership, management, status, or career visibility; affliction still makes career prominent but with pressure, authority conflict, or unstable reputation.
- 11R -> 1st: friends, patrons, groups, hopes, and gains enter the native's life. Dignity gives help from friends and social networks; affliction brings social obligations, unhelpful friends, or disappointed hopes.
- 12R -> 1st: hidden enemies, isolation, hospitals, confinement, mental burden, and secret trouble enter the body/life. If dignified, the native may work beneficially in hidden/medical/charitable/retreat contexts; if afflicted, this is a strong warning for hidden harm, sorrow, psychological pressure, or chronic drains.

## Use With Derived Houses

Flight logic also works from turned houses. Example: the 9th is the 3rd from the 7th, so it can describe spouse's siblings or partner-side communication when the question calls for it. Use turned houses only after the main chart testimony is clear.

## Warnings

- A bad house lord entering the 1st does not automatically mean catastrophe. Require repetition, severe affliction, and relevant timing.
- A good house lord entering a house does not automatically give benefit. It must have dignity, reception, or support.
- Do not use flight logic without checking the planet's natural nature. Mars carrying 2nd-house topics differs from Venus carrying 2nd-house topics.
- When a traditional statement sounds harsh, translate it into a professional consultation judgment unless analyzing a historical case.
