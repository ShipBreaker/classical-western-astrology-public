# Status, Rank, And Overall Grade

Use this when judging 格局, social rank, fame, authority, public success, or whether a chart can support a high level of worldly achievement.

## Rank Is Not A Single Score

Do not judge rank from one dignity table alone. Overall grade comes from the interaction of:

- Ascendant ruler and 1st house
- 10th ruler, MC, and planets in/near the 10th
- Sun and Moon condition
- angularity and strength of benefics/malefics
- sect and whether malefics are constructive or damaging
- aspects/reception between the Ascendant ruler, luminaries, 10th ruler, and MC
- companion stars/attending planets to Sun and Moon when relevant
- environment, family, patronage, institutions, and timing

Achievement is produced by chart plus environment. A dignified planet shows capacity; the life must still have channels through which that capacity can become public.

## Valens-Style Lots For Rank

Valens-style rank checks may use four lots: Fortune, Spirit, Basis, and Exaltation. Use this as additional rank testimony, especially in 格局 questions, not as a replacement for Ascendant, 10th, luminaries, and dignity.

Require exact birth time and sect before calculating. The formulas used in the material are:

- Fortune: day `Asc + Moon - Sun`; night `Asc + Sun - Moon`.
- Spirit: day `Asc + Sun - Moon`; night `Asc + Moon - Sun`.
- Basis: `Asc + Fortune - Spirit` or `Asc + Spirit - Fortune`; use the source's convention if given.
- Exaltation: day `Asc + Aries 19 - Sun`; night `Asc + Taurus 3 - Moon`.

Read the lots mainly by whole-sign relationships:

- A lot or its lord angular from the natal chart strengthens rank testimony.
- A lot lord in the 10th from its own lot, or otherwise angular from its lot, supports prominence.
- A lot lord placed in the sign/house of one of the other rank lots links the system together.
- A lot or lot lord tightly conjunct a bright royal star such as Regulus, Aldebaran, Antares, or Fomalhaut can intensify rank, but only if ordinary chart testimony agrees.
- More repeated testimonies mean stronger rank potential; a single lot testimony is not enough.

Always add the Ascendant ruler and 10th ruler back into the judgment. The lots can show special elevation, but the native still needs a workable body/life channel and public/career channel.

## Luminaries

Sun and Moon are central for judging quality of life and rank.

Check:

- Are the luminaries essentially dignified, in a good sign for sect, or otherwise supported?
- Are they angular or in good houses?
- Are they afflicted by malefics without reception?
- Are they accompanied or supported by strong attending planets?
- Do they connect with Ascendant ruler, 10th ruler, MC, or benefics?

Strong luminaries can elevate the chart, especially when tied to the 1st/10th or supported by benefics. Damaged luminaries can reduce visibility, vitality, family support, or ease of recognition.

## Companion Stars / Doryphory

Companion stars are planets that attend the Sun or Moon and can help them carry rank. Practical rule:

- Better companions are dignified, angular, sect-compatible, and connected to the luminary by meaningful aspect.
- For the Sun, oriental/eastern planets are especially relevant.
- For the Moon, occidental/western planets are especially relevant.
- Benefic companions or dignified malefic companions can support visibility and authority.
- Weak or afflicted companions reduce the luminary's ability to deliver high rank cleanly.

Use this as a refinement, not the first step.

## Ascendant Ruler And 10th Ruler

For fame, office, rank, and public achievement, compare the Ascendant ruler and the 10th ruler.

Key patterns:

- Ascendant ruler applying to 10th ruler: the native seeks rank, office, or public achievement.
- 10th ruler applying to Ascendant ruler: rank, office, superior, or public opportunity comes toward the native.
- Mutual aspect or reception between 1R and 10R: strong career/life integration.
- Angular 1R and 10R: public effect is stronger.
- 1R/10R in good houses with dignity: achievement is easier and more stable.
- 1R/10R in cadent or bad houses: rank may still appear, but with weakness, delay, instability, low origin, or less control.
- 1R with Sun or 10R with Sun can show contact with authority, nobility, leaders, government, or public honor when the rest of the chart supports it.

When the 10th is strong but the 1st is weak, the person may perform important work without feeling personally secure or without high origin. When the 1st is strong but the 10th is weak, talent and identity may be strong but public platform weaker.

## Low Origin And Reverse-Rank Patterns

Do not automatically reject rank because the Ascendant ruler, 10th ruler, or major significator touches the 3rd, 6th, 8th, or 12th. Use "反贵" logic carefully: a muddy origin, hidden house, illness/service/debt/crisis house, or low early environment can become the material through which later rank is built when the core career structure is strong.

Look for recovery factors:

- 1R and 10R applying to each other, receiving each other, or connected by strong benefic reception.
- 10R applying to or receiving 1R: office, public role, or career opportunity comes toward the native.
- Dignified benefics, Sun, or Jupiter lifting an otherwise difficult ruler.
- 11th-house support for patrons, voters, audience, networks, institutions, or public backing.
- 2nd-house support for campaign funds, capital, salary, or resource channels.
- Timing periods that repeatedly activate the same 1R/10R/rank structure.

Bad early life is not the same as low final rank. Separate origin, path difficulty, and eventual office/status.

## Benefics, Malefics, And Sect

A dignified sect-compatible malefic can raise a chart through discipline, law, engineering, administration, military, surgery, construction, or command. A debilitated benefic can give comfort but also indulgence, waste, dependency, or shallow opportunity.

Examples of adjustment:

- Day chart Saturn dignified and above horizon can be a serious structural strength.
- Night chart Mars can be more usable, especially when dignified or received.
- Out-of-sect malefic on angles or harming luminaries/1R/10R requires caution.

## Practical Grade Synthesis

Use this scale internally:

- High-grade public chart: strong 1R/10R/MC/luminaries, angularity, dignity, benefic support, coherent ruler network, repeated rank testimony.
- Skilled but pressured chart: strong malefic or technical planet tied to 1/10/6/3/9, with affliction that produces effort, specialization, or conflict.
- Supported ordinary chart: good 2/4/7/11 or benefics but limited 1/10 testimony.
- Hidden/service chart: strong 6/12/8/3/9 with weak angles; useful work may be behind the scenes.
- Unstable chart: repeated affliction to 1R, luminaries, 10R, and angles without reception or benefic mitigation.

Check whether the status is individual or collective. Strong 9th/10th/11th links can show a teacher, founder, publisher, organizer, or field-builder whose rank comes from gathering people and transmitting a method, not only from solitary visibility.

Always explain the evidence instead of telling the user "high grade" or "low grade" bluntly.
